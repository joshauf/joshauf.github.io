Our Companies

Penske Corporation is a closely-held, diversified, on-highway, transportation services company whose subsidiaries operate in a variety of industry segments, including retail automotive, truck leasing, transportation logistics, transportation component manufacturing and professional motorsports. Penske Corporation manages businesses with revenues in excess of $23 billion, operating in more than 3,300 locations and employs approximately 50,000 people worldwide.

PENSKE AUTOMOTIVE GROUP

Penske Automotive Group is an international transportation services company that operates automotive and commercial truck dealerships principally in the United States, Canada and Western Europe, and distributes commercial vehicles, diesel engines, gas engines, power systems and related parts and services principally in Australia and New Zealand. PAG employs more than 22,000 people worldwide and is a member of the Fortune 500 and Russell 2000.

Team Penske is one of the most successful teams in the history of professional sports. DETROIT GRAND PRIX

The Chevrolet Detroit Belle Isle Grand Prix is a weekend of professional auto racing held at the Raceway at Belle Isle Park in Detroit, Michigan. Roger Penske, founder and chairman of Penske Corporation, championed and invested in the race’s return to Detroit in 2007. Since returning to Detroit, the Detroit Grand Prix has generated over $47 million annually in spending and has helped create over $13 million in improvements to Belle Isle Park.

DAVCO

DAVCO Technology LLC is the North American leader in Class 8 heavy duty diesel powered truck fuel-heater/water separators and filter systems.
