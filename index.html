Tasting Ritual

Professional coffee tasting is often elevated to a fine art, but amateur coffee lovers can enjoy a simplified version of this ritual at home. Get the most from your coffee and enjoy our step by step ritual.

1Observe the crema

VertuoLine coffees are characterized by rich, generous hazelnut-colored foam called crema. Crema is a naturally formed foam of coffee and air produced during the brewing process. Espresso lovers know that crema is the sign of ultimate coffee quality and enhances the tasting pleasure.

2Smell the Aroma

Breathe in the aromas arising from your Grand Cru. At first you'll notice light, floral and fruity scents. Stir the coffee and you will release heavier, roasted woody and spicy notes. The aromas of the roasted coffee hint at the flavor to follow in the cup.

3Taste the flavors

Sip a generous amount of coffee so that the liquid coats your mouth, awakening your taste buds. With this technique, you will experience acidity or liveliness as when tasting a lemon; bitterness as when tasting cocoa; body (thickness of the beverage) in the mouth, and aromas in the nose.

LATTE GOURMAND
Easy5 min.
A variation on a classic Italian Latte Macchiato, the Gourmand adds hot milk to your favorite VertuoLine Coffee while topping with creamy foam for a delightful and tasty treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 capsule any VertuoLine Coffee
  • 4 oz milk

Preparation

  • Fill with 4 oz milk to latte line in the Aeroccino+ frother
  • Run it on hot setting and immediately pour hot frothed milk in VertuoLine Recipe glass
  • Brew Coffee into the glass
COFFEE MACCHIATO
Easy5 min.
Add texture and flavor to your favorite Grand Cru. Hot, frothed milk combines with the finest Coffee to make this macchiato a treat for all the senses.

Ingredients

  • 1 capsule any VertuoLine Coffee
  • 1.5 oz milk

Preparation

  • Brew a VertuoLine Coffee of your choice
  • Prepare 50ml of hot frothed milk with the Aeroccino+ frother
  • Immediately pour hot frothed milk onto the Coffee
COFFEE MILKSHAKE
Easy15 min.
Enjoy a sweet refreshment with this milky delight that promises the rich taste of vanilla and the cool, velvety feel of ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 capsule Decaffeinato or Vanizio
  • 2 oz cold milk
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream (approx. 2 cups ice cream)

Preparation

  • Brew Coffee capsule and allow to cool slightly
  • In a cocktail shaker, add ice cream, cold milk and cooled shot
  • Stir with a whisk or large table spoon
  • Continue until ice cream is melted and mixture is uniformly thickened and smooth
  • Pour into VertuoLine recipe glass
MAPLE CINNAMON COFFEE
Easy15 min.
Like the smell of warm breakfast on a winter morning, this drink balances the traditional flavors of maple syrup and cinnamon with a smooth roast to comfort any inner child.

Ingredients

  • 1 capsule Melozio
  • 4 oz milk
  • 1 oz maple syrup
  • 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbs brown sugar
  • 2 oz cold milk
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream (approx. 2 cups ice cream)

Preparation

  • In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine milk, maple syrup, and cinnamon
  • Whisk until all have combined
  • Continue until brown sugar has dissolved and milk is heated through and foaming
  • Pour into VertuoLine Recipe Glass
  • Pour Coffee onto the milk mixture
  • Note: For an even sweeter and gourmet taste, add 1 tbs brown sugar into the milk mixture
HAZLENUT CHOCOLATE COFFEE
Easy10 min.
The rich flavor of chocolate is the backdrop for the smooth flavors of hazelnut that gives this drink its delicious nutty profile.

Ingredients

  • 1 capsule Hazelino
  • 3 tbs chocolate hazlenut spread
  • 4 oz milk

Preparation

  • In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine milk and chocolate-hazelnut spread
  • Whisk until spread is melted and milk is heated through and foaming
  • Pour hot milk mixture into VertuoLine Recipe glass
  • Brew Coffee onto the mixture
  • Enjoy!

TO USE AEROCCINO+ MILK FROTHER

  • Spoon spread into a large cup
  • Fill milk to latte line
  • Run the Aeroccino+ frother as normal on hot setting
  • Immediately pour hot frothed milk onto hazelnut spread and stir to dissolve spread
  • Pour hot milk mixture into the glass
  • Brew Coffee onto the mixture
  • Enjoy!

ALMOND COFFEE
Easy15 min.
A creamy, milky texture creates the perfect setting for the sweet duet of almond and vanilla, a beautifully delicious harmony of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 capsule Odacio
  • 4 oz milk
  • 2 tbs sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/8 tsp of almond extract
  • 1 vanilla pod (or 1 vanilla bean)

Preparation

  • In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine milks and extracts
  • If using the vanilla pod, split the pod down the center lengthwise
  • Scrape the seeds from the center of the bean into the milk
  • Place split pod into milk as well
  • Whisk until all have combined and milk is heated through and foaming
  • Remove vanilla pod
  • Pour milk mixture in the VertuoLine Recipe glass
  • Brew the Coffee onto the milk mixture
  • Enjoy!
ARANCIATA CAFFE FREDDO
Easy15 min.
The light, fruity notes of Elvazio meet the tangy citrus of orange in this chilled sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 capsule Elvazio
  • 2 tbs orange peel (approx. 3 pieces of sliced peel about 3/4 inch by 1 inch each)
  • 2 tsps sugar or 1 1/2 oz sugar syrup
  • 1/4 cup ice, plus more for glass (1/4 cup)
  • 3 oz blood orange soda
  • Chilled slice of orange, for garnish

Preparation

  • Place orange peel and sugar (or sugar syrup) into heat proof glass
  • Brew Coffee directly over peel and sugar. Stir to dissolve sugar
  • Fill a large (20 oz) cocktail shaker with ice
  • Quickly pour brewed Coffee mixture over ice into cocktail shaker
  • Let sit for 30 seconds to chill
  • With lid on shaker, shake mixture to chill completely
  • Fill a cold VertuoLine Recipe Glass with fresh ice
  • Pour the blood orange soda in glass
  • Gently pour the strained Coffee mixture over a spoon, on the top of soda
  • Garnish with orange slice
INTENSE AND DARK CHOCOLATE COFFEE
Easy10 min.
A dark and powerful taste. The aroma of the Stormio Grand Cru subtly compliments a strong rush of deep cocoa flavoring to create a powerful experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 capsule Stormio
  • 3 oz milk
  • 3 tbs chopped dark chocolate
  • 1.5 tbs raw (Demerara) sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom

Preparation

  • In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine milk and chopped dark chocolate
  • Add in the sugar and cardamom
  • Whisk until all have combined, sugar has dissolved and milk is heated through and foaming
  • Pour milk mixture in the VertuoLine Recipe glass and brew Coffee on it
  • Enjoy!

TO USE AEROCCINO+ MILK FROTHER

  • Spoon spread into a large cup
  • Fill milk to latte line
  • Run the Aeroccino+ frother as normal on hot setting
  • Immediately pour hot frothed milk onto hazelnut spread and stir to dissolve spread
  • Pour hot milk mixture into the glass
  • Brew Coffee onto the mixture
  • Enjoy!
COCONUT COFFEE
Easy10 min.
Capture a slice of island life with this creamy coconut drink that can be served warm as a tropical beach or cool as the rush of ocean waves.

Ingredients

  • 1 capsule Melozio
  • 3/4 oz coconut syrup (1 oz. coconut syrup if making cold preparation)
  • 2 oz milk
  • 2 cups ice (if making cold preparation)

Hot Preparation

  • Froth syrup and milk
  • Brew the Coffee on hot milk mixture in VertuoLine Recipe glass (pictured)

Cold Preparation

  • Brew Coffee into cocktail shaker over ice
  • Stir the ice and the Coffee
  • In VertuoLine Recipe glass mix syrup and milk
  • Pour the Coffee and ice on syrup and milk
  • Enjoy!

  • Brew Coffee into the mixture
  • Enjoy!

SPICED ICED COFFEE
Easy20 min.
Give your ice coffee an extra kick. Feel the warmth of cinnamon and ginger enjoyably contrast the cool refreshment of this summer staple, combined with the luxurious sweetness of honey.

Ingredients

  • 1 capsule Decaffeinato
  • 1 oz good honey
  • 3/4 oz ice
  • 1/8 tsp ginger
  • 1/8 tsp cinnamon

Preparation

  • Spoon honey and spices into heat proof glass, brew coffee into and stir to dissolve
  • Let cool for a few moments
